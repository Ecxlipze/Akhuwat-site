E-ROZGAAR

Developing Skills, Building Dreams

E-ROZGAAR

The Skill Development Program by Tabeer Foundation is a transformative initiative focused on empowering rural youth and women through hands-on vocational training. By offering practical instruction in areas such as handicrafts, stitching, modern farming techniques, and essential IT skills, the program equips participants with the tools they need to thrive in today’s economy.This initiative not only enhances employability but also fosters self-reliance, helping individuals build sustainable livelihoods either through employment or by launching their own small enterprises. By bridging the skills gap in underserved regions, Tabeer Foundation is contributing to long-term economic development and social upliftment at the grassroots level. DONATE
