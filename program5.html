FREE RASHAN PROGRAM

The Free Rashan Program is one of Tabeer Foundation’s core humanitarian initiatives, designed to combat food insecurity among vulnerable and low-income households, particularly in rural and underserved areas. This program aims to provide essential food supplies to families facing economic hardship, helping them meet their basic nutritional needs with dignity and consistency. Through this initiative, Tabeer Foundation distributes carefully selected food packages containing staple items such as flour, rice, pulses, cooking oil, sugar, and other daily necessities. These distributions are conducted regularly, with special emphasis during times of crisis, natural disasters, and religious occasions—when the need for food support is often at its peak.

About us

Tabeer Foundation remains committed to expanding the reach of this program, fostering a stronger, more resilient society where no one is left behind in the fight against hunger.

The Free Rashan Program not only alleviates immediate hunger but also plays a crucial role in reducing the impact of short-term poverty. By ensuring that families do not have to choose between food and other basic needs, the program contributes to improved health, stability, and overall well-being within the communities we serve.
