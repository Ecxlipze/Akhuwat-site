Everything You Need to Know About Making a Difference

Tabeer Foundation for Change is a non‑profit organization registered in Pakistan under Section 42 of the Companies Act (SECP). It was founded in 2024 with a mission to empower underserved rural communities across Pakistan .

The Foundation aims to break the cycle of poverty by promoting financial inclusion, skill development, and welfare programs. Its vision is to enable marginalized rural populations to achieve self‑reliance and long‑term prosperity through education, health, and livelihood support .

Skill Development Program: Vocational training in stitching, handicrafts, IT, farming and more to enhance employability and entrepreneurship . • Interest‑Free Loans: Microcredit for rural individuals to start businesses or improve agriculture, focusing on dignified income generation . • Free Eye Camps: Medical camps providing eye check‑ups, treatment, glasses, and awareness in under‑served areas . • Free Rashan (Food) Program: Distribution of essential food items to low‑income families facing food insecurity . • Free Cloth Program: Seasonal clothing distribution for individuals in remote rural regions . Additionally, shelter homes, small-scale schools, and helpline offices support youth and children in need .

The Foundation primarily focuses on rural areas across Pakistan—regions where poverty is most concentrated. Its initiatives target underserved families, rural youth, women, and marginalized communities for socio-economic upliftment .

Applications can be made through the Foundation’s helpline offices or local field contacts. For specific programs like interest-free loans or vocational training, you may reach out via their contact form, email, or phone to initiate eligibility assessment and application steps

Support options include: • Donations via the website’s “Donate” button. • Sponsorship programs, such as sponsoring a child’s education or vocational training. • Zakat or charitable giving integrated into some initiatives. Transparent usage of funds is emphasized, and donors may receive progress updates on supported cases .

Their main office is based in Lahore, Pakistan. You can contact them via: • Email: info@tabeerfoundation.org • Phone: 03020504375 • Contact form: On the “Contact” page of the website .

Though specific figures may vary, the Foundation highlights achievements such as: • Training thousands in vocational skills • Distributing food, clothing, and medical aid in remote areas • Assisting rural youth through shelter homes, schools, and loans Exact numbers are featured on the site under “Our numbers that speak” .
